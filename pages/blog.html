The Dhoni Touch

"Dhoni Touch: The Perfect Mahi" is a biography about one of India's most iconic and successful cricketers, Mahendra Singh Dhoni. Authored by Bharat Sundaresan, a renowned cricket writer, the book delves deep into the life and career of Dhoni, exploring his journey from a small-town boy to becoming the captain of the Indian cricket team and leading it to numerous victories on the international stage. The title "Dhoni Touch" likely refers to the unique qualities and skills that Dhoni possessed both on and off the field, often referred to as his 'magic touch' or 'captain cool' demeanor. The book aims to uncover the various facets of Dhoni's personality, his leadership style, his approach to the game, and the impact he has had on Indian cricket. Throughout the pages of "Dhoni Touch," readers can expect to find anecdotes, insights, and behind-the-scenes stories about Dhoni's life, his rise to stardom, his tactical brilliance, as well as his contributions to Indian cricket's golden era. The book provides a comprehensive look at Dhoni's achievements, challenges, and the legacy he leaves behind in the world of cricket. Overall, "Dhoni Touch" offers fans and cricket enthusiasts an engaging and intimate portrayal of one of the most celebrated figures in Indian cricket history, showcasing the essence of Dhoni's unparalleled influence and the mark he has left on the sport.

Bharat Sundaresan, 2018
